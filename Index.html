Projects


My most notable projects, displayed here.

GrowIt

A small, portable greenhouse that can grow fruits and vegetables on it's own. Me and my team created this with 3 arduino's and a bunch of sensors. The greenhouse knows what kind of fruit/vegetable is inside and adjusts the temperature, lighting and water supply accordingly

Spotify country battle

Choose 2 countries and let them battle with music. I used the spotify API to collect music data from all countries that support Spotify and the outcome is based on number of views/listens of the most popular track of that country.

Slimme Slaper / Smart Sleeper

This was a very fun project to do. We made a bed that records the user's movement with an arduino and a bunch of sensors (accelerometer did the most important work) and it calculates the best time to wake up, it also has a speaker that plays the star wars theme song as alarm sound. The user sets an alarm and the arduino will start calculating 30 minutes before the alarm goes off. We researched deep and light sleep and sleepcycles to get the most accurate calculation.

Luna's Adventure

This was the most difficult project to do because 3 out of 5 team members quit and I literally did all of the coding and I drew all the artwork. We made an educational game for 10 year old children. A picture is displayed on the right and the goal of the game is to put letters in the correct order, the letters need to form the word that corresponds with the picture.
